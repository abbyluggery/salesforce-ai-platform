## Recipe Notes

### Sodium Reduction Tips
1. Always use low-sodium broths and soy sauce
2. Make homemade seasoning mixes (avoid pre-packaged)
3. Use fresh or no-salt-added canned vegetables
4. Limit cheese to 1-2 oz per serving
5. Replace salt with herbs, spices, lemon, vinegar

### Heart-Healthy Modifications
1. Olive oil instead of butter in most recipes
2. Greek yogurt instead of sour cream or heavy cream
3. Baking/grilling instead of frying
4. 93% lean ground meat
5. Reduced-fat cheese in moderation

### Diabetic-Friendly Tips
1. Brown rice instead of white rice
2. Whole wheat pasta instead of regular
3. Control portion sizes of carbohydrates
4. Balance every meal with protein and vegetables
5. Use whole grain tortillas and breads

### Meal Prep Friendly Recipes
- Soups and stews (freeze well)
- Slow cooker meals (batch cooking)
- Sheet pan dinners (easy scaling)
- Overnight oats and breakfast burritos
- Mason jar salads and Buddha bowls
