## CORE CONCEPT

### The Problem You're Solving

**For Neurodivergent Job Seekers:**
- Overwhelming executive function burden of searching multiple sites daily
- Decision paralysis from too many options and steps
- Burnout from repetitive, scattered searching (Indeed, LinkedIn, company sites)
- Hidden shame of feeling "lazy" when the system wasn't built for their brains
- Mental health challenges affecting job search (mood cycling, anxiety, imposter syndrome)

**Market Gap Identified:**
- **Existing automation tools** (LazyApply, LoopCV, Sonara, JobCopilot): Focus on application speed, not cognitive overwhelm
- **Neurodivergent job platforms** (Mentra, Neuro Hire Network): Focus on job matching, not automation
- **Missing:** Automation designed specifically for executive function challenges

### Your Solution

**Unified Job Search Platform that:**
1. Aggregates jobs from multiple sources overnight
2. Filters based on neurodivergent-specific criteria (skills, sensory needs, company culture)
3. Delivers personalized morning summary reports
4. Removes the cognitive burden of daily site-hopping
5. Integrates mental health support for job search anxiety
6. Provides executive function support (task breaking, decision-making help)

**Emotion Tracking:**
